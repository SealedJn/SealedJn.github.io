ChatGPT+NewBing 国内试用（问题：Something went Wrong, Please try it agin)

2023-02-19

本篇文章的目的

为了尝试chatgpt，学习知识（报效祖国），相信很多人都遇到了Something went Wrong, Please try it agin的问题。
本人的方法不一定适用所有人，若能帮助到大家，吾不胜荣幸。

引用brant_liu的回答

To my personal experience. The problem is about the Bing rewards program. I heard
from others that the user can get 200 points automaticly by changing their
location. >Maybe you don't do it on purpose, but switching the proxies do help gain
the 'extra' point. The first step, quit the Being rewards program at https://
rewards.bing.com/optout. The second step, Rejoin the Bing rewards program at
https://rewards.bing.com/. Finally, just clear your browser cache And retry.
--By brant_liu on Feb 17, 2023 [https://answers.microsoft.com/en-us/bing/forum/all/new-bing-always-something-went-wrong-please-try/739714f7-8ca7-4367-8a72-84e05bb85d7d]

基本步骤

1、更改你的国家区域[https://account.microsoft.com/profile]，推荐改成其他国家，你懂的。【Except China】

2、如果你已经加入了Bing rewards，则退出[https://rewards.bing.com/optout]。未加入则忽略这一步
3、再次加入Bing rewards[https://rewards.bing.com/]。
4、清除浏览器缓存（若使用的是Micriosoft Edge，则可以参考：[edge://settings/privacy]），退出浏览器后，再重新进入加入Bing wait list的页面[https://www.bing.com/search?q=I%20need%20to%20throw%20a%20dinner%20party%20for%206%20people%20who%20are%20vegetarian.%20Can%20you%20suggest%20a%203-course%20menu%20with%20a%20chocolate%20dessert?&iscopilotedu=1&form=MA13G9]
